Privacy Policy

At ISKA University, we value and respect your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, apply to our programs, or engage with our university in any other way.

  • Information Collection: We collect personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, and academic records when you submit forms, request information, or register for services.
  • Usage of Data: The personal data we collect is used to provide educational services, process admissions, and improve your experience with our university.
  • Cookies: Our website uses cookies to enhance functionality, analyze website traffic, and improve your experience. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies.
  • Data Sharing: ISKA University will never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law or to provide necessary services.
  • Security Measures: We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.
  • Your Rights: You have the right to access, update, and delete your personal information at any time. For any inquiries regarding your data, please contact our Data Protection Officer.

For more detailed information, read our full Privacy Policy.
