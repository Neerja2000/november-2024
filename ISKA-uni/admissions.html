How to Apply

Follow these steps to complete your application process:

1. Create an Account

Start by creating your account with a valid email address. Use a non-school email to ensure long-term accessibility.

2. Fill Out the Application

Complete all sections, including personal details, academic history, and activities. Save your progress as you go.

3. Submit Your Application

Review your application carefully and submit it before the deadline. Keep a copy of the confirmation receipt.
