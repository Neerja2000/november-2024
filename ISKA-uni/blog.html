ISKA University: A Hub of Celebration and Spirit

At ISKA University, we believe in creating a vibrant and inclusive campus culture that goes beyond academics. Our students not only excel in their studies but also celebrate life, culture, and community. Through a variety of events throughout the year, we foster creativity, collaboration, and school spirit. Here's a look at some of the exciting celebrations and milestones that make ISKA University a special place.

Oct 02, 2023
Youth Festival: A Celebration of Talent and Creativity

Every year, ISKA University hosts its much-awaited Youth Festival, a celebration of the energy, passion, and creativity that define our student body. This event showcases a wide range of activities, including:

  • Music and Dance Performances: Talented students take the stage to perform traditional and contemporary music, dance, and theater.
  • Art Exhibitions: Students display their visual art creations, from paintings to digital art, reflecting the diverse talents of the university.
  • Sports Competitions: Friendly yet fierce competition in sports and games helps promote teamwork and physical fitness.

The Youth Festival is not just about showcasing talent but also about bringing students together to enjoy a festive and fun-filled atmosphere.

April 02, 2023
Sports Day: Where Team Spirit Shines

Sports Day at ISKA University is a much-loved tradition where students, staff, and faculty come together to participate in friendly sporting events. From athletics to team sports like football and volleyball, the day is filled with excitement and competitive spirit. The event is not just about winning but also about fostering teamwork, building relationships, and encouraging a healthy lifestyle.

Dec 27, 2023
Christmas and New Year Celebrations: A Time for Family and Festivity

The end of the year is a time for reflection, joy, and togetherness at ISKA University. Each year, we celebrate the Christmas and New Year festivities with a series of special events that include:

  • Christmas Tree Lighting Ceremony: Students and staff gather to light the grand Christmas tree, spreading festive cheer across the campus.
  • Holiday Parties: The university hosts fun-filled holiday parties with music, food, and games, creating a warm atmosphere for students to unwind and celebrate.
  • New Year’s Eve Gala: A spectacular event with live performances, dinner, and a countdown to ring in the new year together.

The Youth Festival is not just about showcasing talent but also about bringing students together to enjoy a festive and fun-filled atmosphere.
