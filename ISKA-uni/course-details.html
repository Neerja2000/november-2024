Academic Program

Undergraduate Programs

Begin Your Journey at ISKA University and Build a Solid Foundation

Course Overview

Our Undergraduate programs provide students with a solid foundation for a successful career. From engineering to business studies, we offer diverse courses designed to cater to the ever-changing demands of the global job market. Our program ensures that each student is well-prepared with knowledge and practical skills needed to succeed.

Course Objectives
  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills applicable in real-world scenarios.
  • Equip students with the ability to manage tasks, deadlines, and resources effectively.
  • Enhance leadership, communication, and teamwork abilities, preparing students for global career opportunities.
  • Offer exposure to a diverse set of academic fields and industries.
Course Duration

The Undergraduate program spans 4 years, divided into 8 semesters. Each semester blends theoretical learning with practical application, offering students both in-class and hands-on experience.

Prerequisites
  • High School Diploma (or equivalent) with strong marks in relevant subjects.
  • Basic understanding of mathematics and science (depending on course choice).
  • English language proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL or equivalent) is required for non-native speakers.
Faculty and Department Information

The course is delivered by highly experienced faculty members who bring both academic expertise and industry experience. The department offers state-of-the-art facilities, including research labs, online resources, and excellent student support services.

Enrollment Options and Tuition Fees

Enrollment is open year-round. Students can apply directly online or visit the admissions office. The tuition fee for the program is $10,000 per year, with additional costs for materials, activities, and international trips.

Note: Scholarships and financial aid are available for qualified students.
