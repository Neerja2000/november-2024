Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Is there an entrance exam for admissions?

Yes, most programs require you to take an entrance exam. Details can be found under the Admissions Guidelines section.

What are the eligibility criteria for undergraduate programs?

Eligibility criteria vary by program. Generally, students must have completed high school with a minimum percentage specified for their chosen course.

Does the university offer online courses?

Yes, several programs and certifications are available online. Check the Online Learning section for more details.

What sports facilities are available?

We have a gym, swimming pool, and courts for basketball, tennis, and football. Check the Campus Life section for more.
