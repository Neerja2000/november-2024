About us
ISKA University's History

ISKA University began as a small academic institution with a vision to revolutionize education and inspire future leaders. Over the years, it has grown into a globally recognized university, renowned for its innovative research, diverse academic programs, and inclusive community. With a foundation rooted in excellence and a commitment to empowering students, ISKA has nurtured countless graduates who have made significant contributions across industries and societies worldwide. Our legacy is built upon a tradition of intellectual curiosity, collaboration, and a drive to address the pressing challenges of our time.

ISKA University's Mission

Our mission at ISKA University is to provide quality education that inspires and empowers students to achieve their goals. We are committed to creating a learning environment that fosters knowledge, innovation, and leadership. By focusing on academic excellence and community development, we aim to contribute to the growth and success of Angola and the world.

ISKA University's Vision

Our vision is to become a beacon of education in Angola and globally, offering cutting-edge programs that cater to diverse learning needs. We strive to integrate technology with traditional teaching methods to make education accessible to all. Through partnerships with industry leaders and a commitment to academic excellence, ISKA University envisions shaping future leaders and innovators who will make a lasting impact in their communities and beyond.
